# MfalmeGroom

**Caption:** Where every man becomes a king

MfalmeGroom is a professional grooming website offering premium services to help clients look and feel their best. The platform provides a smooth online booking experience, clear service descriptions, a gallery of previous work, and client testimonials.  

---

## Services Offered

### **1. Haircut & Styling**
- **Classic & Modern Haircuts** – Fades, tapers, trims, and clean cuts.  
- **Beard Shaping & Grooming** – Line-ups, trimming, shaping, and styling.  
- **Styling Consultation** – Personalized advice to help clients choose the best look.

### **2. Massage & Relaxation**
- **Swedish Massage** – Gentle massage for stress relief and relaxation.  
- **Deep Tissue Massage** – Focuses on muscle tension and deeper pressure points.  
- **Hot Stone & Aromatherapy** – Warm stones and essential oils for enhanced relaxation.

### **3. Manicure & Pedicure**
- **Nail Trimming & Shaping** – Clean grooming for hands and feet.  
- **Cuticle Care & Buffing** – Promotes healthy cuticles and smooth nail surfaces.  
- **Polish or Natural Shine** – Option for clear polish or a simple natural finish.

### **4. Spa & Skincare**
- **Facial Treatments** – Cleansing, exfoliation, masks, and moisturizing.  
- **Body Scrubs & Polish** – Full-body exfoliation to refresh the skin.  
- **Body Wraps & Rejuvenation** – Hydrating and detoxifying wraps for full-body refreshment.

---

## Features

- **Online Booking System**  
  Users can book appointments using Flask forms connected to a MySQL database.

- **Services Pricing Page**  
  A full breakdown of service prices for easy planning.

- **Gallery of Previous Work**  
  Images showcasing past haircuts, spa treatments, and grooming sessions.

- **Testimonials**  
  Reviews from satisfied clients highlighting their experience.

- **Admin Dashboard**  
  A backend panel to manage bookings, feedback, and service updates.

## Color Theme

**Background Colors:**  
- Black: `#000000`  
- Dark Navy: `#0B1F3B`  
- Chocolate: `#7B3F00`  

**Text Colors:**  
- Yellow: `#FFD700`  
- Green: `#32CD32`  
- Red: `#FF4500`  
- White: `#FFFFFF`  

**Usage Guide:**  
- Headings: Yellow (`#FFD700`)  
- Highlights / Subheadings: Green (`#32CD32`)  
- Important notes / warnings: Red (`#FF4500`)  
- Body text: White (`#FFFFFF`)  
- Background: Choose between Black, Dark Navy, or Chocolate per page or section.

This palette creates a bold, masculine, and modern aesthetic while keeping all content readable and visually balanced.
